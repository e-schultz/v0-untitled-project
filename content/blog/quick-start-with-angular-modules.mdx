However, I experienced a sinking feeling after reading several pages of Breaking Changes about the introduction of <span class="ds-code-highlight">@NgModule</span> and changing how we need to bootstrap our applications.</p><p>To be fair, the Angular team did a good job of providing examples of before and after code as part of those changes but it was still a lot to process. The process ended up being easier than expected. To my surprise, the application booted up with only a few deprecation warnings in the console.</p><p>In RC5, the Angular 2 team has left many of the deprecated features in place - providing you with warnings instead. Under the hood, they are hoisting things into <span class="ds-code-highlight">NgModule</span> for you.
